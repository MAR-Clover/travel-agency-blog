Top 5 things to do in Kyoto:

  1. Visit Fushimi Inari Shrine: Famous for its thousands of vibrant red torii gates that wind through the forest, Fushimi Inari Taisha is a must-see. The gates lead up Mount Inari, offering stunning views of the city.
  2. Explore Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion): This iconic Zen temple is covered in gold leaf and set against a picturesque pond and garden. It's one of Kyoto's most photographed sites.
  3. Stroll Through Arashiyama Bamboo Grove: Wander through towering bamboo stalks in this magical forest. The nearby Arashiyama area is also home to the scenic Togetsukyo Bridge and several temples.
  4. Visit Kiyomizu-dera Temple: Perched on a hillside, Kiyomizu-dera offers breathtaking views of Kyoto. The templeâ€™s wooden stage, supported by hundreds of pillars, is especially famous during cherry blossom season.
  5. Experience Gion District: Kyoto's famous geisha district, Gion, offers a glimpse of traditional Japanese culture with its teahouses, old wooden buildings, and narrow streets. You may even spot a geisha or maiko (apprentice geisha) walking to their appointments.
